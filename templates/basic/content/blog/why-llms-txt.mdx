# Why your site needs llms.txt

AI agents (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Cursor) don't want your JS-rendered homepage. They want a clean, compact index of your content — who you are, what you publish, and how to find it.

`llms.txt` is that index. Think `robots.txt`, but for understanding instead of crawling.

## What it looks like

```
# My Company

> What we do, in one sentence.

- [Home](/): overview
- [Pricing](/pricing): three tiers from $9

## Blog

- [Hello world](/blog/hello-world): first post
- [Why llms.txt](/blog/why-llms-txt): this post
```

## What it gets you

- Agents answer questions about your site without hallucinating
- Your content lands in agent answers with the right links
- Token costs drop — agents pull targeted pages instead of full HTML
