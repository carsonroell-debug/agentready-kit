# Hello, world

This is the first post on a site built with the [AgentReady Kit](https://agentready.tools/kit).

Every post you write goes in `content/blog/*.mdx`. The Kit handles the rest:

- Renders at `/blog/<slug>`
- Appears in `/llms.txt` under **Blog**
- Has a `<AgentMetadata>` JSON-LD block
- Is accessible as pure Markdown at `/blog/<slug>.md`

## What to do next

1. Edit `agentready.config.ts` — name, description, base URL.
2. Replace this post with your own.
3. Deploy to Vercel.
